About Us

Kopi Btiga is a cozy and warm place to enjoy a wide selection of coffee, tea and snacks. We are located in Kp. Pala Kresek Ds. Patrasana Balaraja-Tangerang, and are open every day except Thursday from late evening to night. Kopi Btiga is a great place to relax, work, study, or gather with friends and family. We always strive to provide friendly, fast, and professional service to all our customers. We hope you can feel a pleasant and refreshing atmosphere in Kopi Btiga. We look forward to your visit!
